Return & Refund Policy

At SpikeList, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction with every purchase. If you are not fully satisfied, our return and exchange policy is outlined below.

1. Applicable Region

This return policy applies to orders shipped within the United States.

2. Returns Eligibility

We accept returns under the following conditions:

- Returns are accepted for both defective and non-defective products

- Products must be unused and in new condition

- Return requests must be initiated within 7 days of delivery

3. Exchanges

We gladly accept product exchanges within the same 7-day return window, subject to product availability.

4. Return Methods

You can return your product using one of the following methods:

- In-store return

- Drop-off at a designated return location

For assistance with return locations or instructions, please visit:

https://shop.spikelist.com/return-poilcy.html

5. Restocking Fee

A 5% restocking fee will be applied to all eligible returns. This fee will be deducted from your refund amount.

6. Refund Process

- Once your returned item is received and inspected, your refund will be processed

- Refunds will be issued to the original payment method

- Please allow up to 30 days for the refund to be completed

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or need help with your return, please reach out through our contact page:

https://shop.spikelist.com/contact.html
