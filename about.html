Our Story

Powering Businesses with the Right IT

SpikeList was founded with a clear mission: make enterprise-grade IT hardware accessible, affordable, and hassle-free for businesses of every size across India.

We're Not Just a Store

SpikeList began in 2018 as a small reseller in Pune, Maharashtra, serving local businesses with honest advice and dependable hardware. We grew by doing one thing exceptionally well — putting our customers first.

Today, we serve clients across 20+ cities, ranging from early-stage startups to established enterprises. Our team of certified IT specialists helps organizations build the right infrastructure from day one.

Every product on our platform is vetted, authorized, and backed by full manufacturer warranty. We're not just selling boxes — we're building long-term technology partnerships.
